## Information We Collect

### Personal Information

When you create an account, we collect:
- **Name** - For personalization and account identification
- **Email address** - For account access, communication, and password recovery
- **Profile information** - Optional bio, institution, and preferences
- **Payment information** - Processed securely through Stripe (we do not store full credit card numbers)

### Educational Content

When you use our Service, we collect:
- **Uploaded files** - Audio recordings, PDFs, and documents you upload for processing
- **Generated content** - Flashcards, summaries, and study materials created from your uploads
- **Study data** - Progress, quiz scores, review history, and spaced repetition metrics
- **Course information** - Courses you create and materials you organize

### Usage Information

We automatically collect:
- **Log data** - IP address, browser type, device information, and operating system
- **Usage patterns** - Features used, time spent, pages visited, and interaction data
- **Performance data** - Error logs, crash reports, and service diagnostics
- **Cookies and tracking** - Session management and analytics (see Cookies section)

## Educational Data Protection

### FERPA Compliance

For U.S. educational institutions, we comply with the Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act (FERPA):
- We act as a school official with legitimate educational interest
- Student data is used solely for educational purposes
- We do not share student data without proper authorization
- Institutions maintain control over student records

### International Students

For users in the European Union, we comply with GDPR:
- Lawful basis for processing (contract, consent, legitimate interest)
- Data minimization and purpose limitation
- Right to access, rectification, erasure, and portability
- Right to object and restrict processing

For users in California, we comply with CCPA:
- Right to know what data is collected
- Right to delete personal information
- Right to opt out of data sales (we do not sell data)
- Non-discrimination for exercising privacy rights
