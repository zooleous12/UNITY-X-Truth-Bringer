## THE 4-COLOR ROCKER SWITCH

### Location
- Position: Left side, adjacent to Left Shift key
- Form: 4-directional rocker (N/S/E/W) or 4-segment circular pad
- Tactile: Distinct click feedback per direction
- LED Backlight: Color-coded per direction
