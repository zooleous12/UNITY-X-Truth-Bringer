## 1. Purpose

## 3. Causation Analysis: From Technical Sabotage to Academic Withdrawal

The documented technical sabotage was the direct and proximate cause of Mr. Kendrick's forced withdrawal from the Spring 2026 academic semester at Kansas State University. The persistent and severe system instability, culminating in the late-February discovery of the BBSK malware, made it impossible to complete coursework, participate in online classes, or prepare for final examinations. The time required to conduct a deep forensic investigation and remediate the firmware-level infection consumed the critical end-of-semester period, leaving no viable path to academic recovery.
